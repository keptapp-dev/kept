Kept — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 7, 2026

Overview

Kept ("the App") is a personal promise-keeping and confidence journal. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Kept does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information you enter — including your name, promises, reflections, rewards, and settings — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. No data is shared with third parties.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are processed entirely on-device and do not involve any external push notification servers beyond the standard platform services provided by Apple (APNs) or Google (FCM).

Photos

If you choose to use a personal photo as a background, the image remains on your device and is never uploaded or shared.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Reset" option in the App's Settings screen. Uninstalling the App also removes all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
